Resume Formatting

✅ Do's
  • Use a clean, professional font (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
  • Keep font size between 10-12 points
  • Use consistent formatting throughout
  • Maintain proper margins (0.5-1 inch)
  • Use bullet points for easy scanning
  • Keep it to 1-2 pages maximum
❌ Don'ts
  • Don't use fancy fonts or colors
  • Avoid text smaller than 10pt
  • Don't overcrowd the page
  • Avoid using tables or complex layouts
  • Don't use graphics or images
  • Never exceed 3 pages

Content Strategy

Key Principles
Be Specific

Use specific numbers, percentages, and metrics to quantify your achievements.

Good: "Increased sales by 25% in Q3"
Bad: "Responsible for sales"
Action-Oriented

Start bullet points with strong action verbs.

Achieved Developed Implemented Led
Relevant

Tailor your resume for each job application.

Focus on experiences that match the job requirements.
Recent

Emphasize recent and relevant experiences.

Last 10-15 years are most important.
